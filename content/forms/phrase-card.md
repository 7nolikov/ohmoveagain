**Instructions:** Print and carry this card to your MUP, FINA, Porezna uprava, and HZZO appointments. Show the relevant section to the officer if you cannot explain in Croatian or English.

---

## At MUP (Ministry of Interior)

| Croatian | English |
|---|---|
| Trebam registrirati boravište, molim. | I need to register my place of residence, please. |
| Imam termin za ___. | I have an appointment for ___. |
| Trebam dozvolu boravka. | I need a residence permit. |
| Gdje je šalter za strance? | Where is the foreigners' counter? |
| Imam sva potrebna dokumenta. | I have all the required documents. |
| Trebam tumača, molim. | I need an interpreter, please. |
| Mogu li dobiti potvrdu? | Can I get a confirmation receipt? |

---

## At FINA / Porezna uprava (OIB)

| Croatian | English |
|---|---|
| Trebam OIB, molim. | I need a personal identification number (OIB), please. |
| Jesam li na popisu za OIB? | Am I registered to receive an OIB? |
| Nisam državljanin Hrvatske. | I am not a Croatian citizen. |
| Imam dozvolu boravka. | I have a residence permit. |

---

## At HZZO (Health Insurance)

| Croatian | English |
|---|---|
| Trebam prijaviti zdravstveno osiguranje. | I need to enrol in health insurance. |
| Gdje mogu dobiti obrazac T-1? | Where can I get the T-1 form? |
| Jesam li prijavljen/prijavljena kao osiguranik? | Am I registered as an insured person? |
| Trebam iskaznicu zdravstvenog osiguranja. | I need a health insurance card. |

---

## At the bank

| Croatian | English |
|---|---|
| Trebam otvoriti račun, molim. | I need to open an account, please. |
| Imam OIB i dozvolu boravka. | I have an OIB and residence permit. |
| Koji su uvjeti za otvaranje računa? | What are the conditions for opening an account? |
| Na koji IBAN da pošaljem uplatu? | To which IBAN should I send a transfer? |

---

## General

| Croatian | English |
|---|---|
| Ne govorim hrvatski. | I do not speak Croatian. |
| Govorite li engleski? | Do you speak English? |
| Molim, polako. | Please, slowly. |
| Razumijem. | I understand. |
| Ne razumijem. | I don't understand. |
| Hvala. | Thank you. |
| Gdje je ___? | Where is ___? |
| Koliko dugo traje? | How long does it take? |
