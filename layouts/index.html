Moving to Croatia · a sourced, step-by-step playbook

Relocate to Croatia without guessing.

A practical, open-source guide through the five stages of a Croatia move — what to do, in what order, which documents you need, and where the official source says so. Every claim is linked to a government or EU source with a verified date.

I built this while moving to Croatia myself. The official portals are fragmented, the blog posts skip the hard parts, and I kept hitting the same questions: which visa, what order, which documents, by when? This is the playbook I wish I had — now open-source so it stays accurate.

Why this exists

Most relocation information is scattered across official portals you have to piece together yourself, or buried in blog posts that skip the hard questions. This site is an open-source, community-maintained playbook: every claim links to an official source with a verified date, anyone can open an issue to flag something stale, and pull requests are welcome.
