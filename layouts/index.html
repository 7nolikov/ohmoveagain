Why this exists

Most relocation information is either an official portal you have to stitch together yourself, or a blog post that skips the uncomfortable questions. This site is an open-source, community-maintained playbook: every claim is linked to an official source with an "as of" date, and anyone can open an issue to flag something out-of-date or submit a pull request to fix it.
